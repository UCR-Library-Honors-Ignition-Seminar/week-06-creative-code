# Week 6 Assignment: Creative Code & Games

**Due:** End of Week 6 | **Late policy:** 20% deduction; accepted through end of Week 7

---

## Project

Choose **one** of the following:

### Option A: p5.js Sketch
Create a generative sketch that:
- Is **generative** — produces different output each run or changes over time
- Is **intentional** — has a concept behind it (theme, mood, question)
- Runs for at least 30 seconds

Share via p5.js Web Editor share link (Sketch > Share > Copy link). Save in `links.md`.

### Option B: Bitsy Game
Build a complete game with:
- At least **3 rooms**
- At least **1 NPC or sprite with dialogue**
- A clear beginning and some sense of ending or resolution
- A title screen (make your first room a title screen)

Export as HTML and commit to your repo.

---

## Reflection

Write a **~300-word reflection** using the [reflection template](reflection-template.md).

**This week's critical prompt (Part 3):**

> *"How does working within the constraints of p5.js or Bitsy change what you make? What would be impossible in this tool — and does that matter?"*
